**Overview**

Categories are used to classify and group similar processes within different categories that can be created whether a process was created or not. More than one category can be created for different processes but only one category may be assigned by process.

Creating a Category

Create a new category by clicking on the "New" button, on the new window enter the name of the new category and then click on the "Save" button:
